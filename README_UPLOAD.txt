WHY FLIPPED MATH
----------------
Flipped Math provides a complete, free, public AP Precalculus sequence organized by the official topic numbering.
Flamingo Math is also strong, but most course lesson links require enrollment, so Flipped Math is more reliable for a public student portal.

After GitHub Pages deploys, press Ctrl+F5 once to clear the old cached JavaScript.
